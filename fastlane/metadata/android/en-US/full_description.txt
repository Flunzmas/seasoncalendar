<p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul>
<li>Boasts the biggest collection of foods</li>
<li>Lets you pick and view your favorites</li>
<li>Can be run on a variety of android devices</li>
<li>Looks and feels better than most competitor apps (my personal opinion)</li></ul>
